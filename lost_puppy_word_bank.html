The Lost Puppy Word Bank

Key Vocabulary

  • Max

    The name of the puppy, a golden retriever.

  • Golden Retriever

    A breed of dog known for its fluffy fur.

  • Town

    The small setting where the story takes place.

  • Explore

    To wander and discover new places, as Max does.

  • Sunny

    The weather during Max’s adventure.

  • Butterflies

    Insects Max chases while wandering.

  • Flowers

    Plants Max sniffs during his adventure.

  • Whiskers

    The name of the friendly cat Max meets.

  • Scared

    How Max feels when he realizes he is lost.

  • Lily

    The kind girl who helps Max find his way home.

  • Comfort

    To soothe or reassure, as Lily does for Max.

  • Name Tag

    An item on Max’s collar with his owner’s contact information.

  • Owner

    The person Max belongs to and returns to.

  • Barking

    The sound Max makes when joyful.

  • Lesson

    Something learned, like Max staying close to home.
